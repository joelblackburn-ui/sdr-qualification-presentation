BANT

Outdated & Seller-Centric
B

Do they have the funds?
A
Authority
Decision-maker or gatekeeper?

Specific pain point?
T
Timeline
When do they need it?

2026 Reality: BANT is not customer-centric and doesn't prioritise the buyer's journey. Don't lead with Budget - most don't have a "line item" budget until after they see value.

Use When...

  • High-velocity SMB environment
  • Transactional, low price point (<$10k/year)
  • Need to triage leads instantly
  • Speed is more important than depth

Avoid When...

  • Modern SaaS (no pre-demo budget)
  • Strategic cold outbound
  • Complex buying committees

Key Insight: Start with business goals, not your product. Understand their strategic direction first.

Use When...

  • Consultative mid-market/enterprise sales
  • Strategic accounts with long cycles
  • Selling transformation or major initiatives

Avoid When...

  • Transactional, quick-turn sales
  • Prospects who don't know their goals
  • Simple product purchases

Qualification in the Age of AI

2026 Trends Reshaping SDR Qualification:

💡

Intent Signals

See who's researching your category before even picking up the phone. AI surfaces warm leads proactively.

🤖

AI Co-Pilots

Real-time transcription tools (Gong, Chorus) flag qualification gaps during the call, not after.

💻

Digital SDRs

AI agents handle Tier 3 inbound leads 24/7, freeing humans for Tier 1 strategic accounts.

🎯

Hyper-Personalization

Qualification isn't a script anymore - it's a conversation fueled by data insights and context.

The Shift: AI handles the "checking boxes" so you can focus on being human and building relationships.

The Perfect SDR-to-AE Handoff

1

No Data Gaps

Every qualification field in the CRM must be populated. No blank fields, no guessing.

2

The Story

Don't just list facts - tell the AE why the prospect is excited. Context matters.

3

Warm Introduction

Join the first 5 minutes of the discovery call to bridge the relationship personally.

4

"Why Now?"

The most important question: Why are they talking to us today instead of six months ago?

The Gold Standard: The AE should feel like they already know the prospect before the first call.

⚠️
The "Sales Crime" Warning

In 2026, the biggest mistake is Interrogative Selling.
If your qualification feels like a checklist to the prospect, they will shut down.

The Fix

Layer your qualification into the natural flow of the conversation.
Use AI co-pilots to "check the boxes" in the background so you can focus on being human.

Summary & Best Practices

1

The 70/30 Rule

Listen more than you talk. The best qualifiers are the best listeners.

2

Be a Consultant

Ask the hard questions that make the prospect think. Don't be a vendor.

3

Disqualify Early

Your time is your most valuable asset. If it's a "No," make it a "No" quickly.

4

Iterate & Adapt

Methodologies aren't static. Adapt them to your specific industry and persona.

Remember: The methodology is a guide, not a script. The goal is qualified conversations, not completed checklists.
